What LabContext is

LabContext explains laboratory medicine—between memorization and lived understanding.

LabContext is interpretive, not evaluative. It exists to make hidden logic visible: why systems behave the way they do, why patterns recur, and why those patterns change when constraints shift. It does not teach procedures, provide validation protocols, or perform competency training.
