What LabContext will not do

These boundaries protect clarity and prevent false authority.

Concept Boundary

No step-by-step workflows, instrument menus, validation protocols, or task execution guidance. Procedures are local, context-bound, and safety-critical.

Concept Boundary

No scoring, progress tracking, “readiness” framing, or exam-optimization language. Understanding is treated as model-building, not a performance target.
