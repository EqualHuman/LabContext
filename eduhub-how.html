How to use the Education Hub

This is built like a reference library. You can enter through any department hub, concept page, or cross-link. Pages are designed to be readable in isolation.

Common entry points

A result looks wrong

Open Constraints for that department to see common distortions and missing context.

A pattern repeats

Open Patterns to see what moves together and what tends to diverge.

You need the baseline logic

Open Foundations for the definitions, measurement logic, and mental models.

You are cross-checking departments

Open Interfaces to connect shared signals across systems.

LabContext
A clarification authority

LabContext is interpretive, not evaluative. It exists to make hidden logic visible: why systems behave the way they do, why patterns recur, and why those patterns change when constraints shift. It does not teach procedures, provide validation protocols, or perform competency training.
